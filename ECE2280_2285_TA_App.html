ECE 2280/2285 Teaching Assistant
Position Application

The ECE department is now considering applications for Teaching Assistant positions. Teaching Assistants receive an Experiential Learning Scholarship ($12 per hour) and are assigned to help students complete course and lab tasks as well as hold study sessions and office hours. Teaching Assistants do NOT grade students' reports or homework.

Being a Teaching Assistant has several advantages, such as enhancing your resume when applying for jobs, deepening your understanding of the material through teaching/troubleshooting, and improving your communication skills by helping other students.

Please let us know if you are interested in the TA positions by taking the survey below. TAs are selected based on academic performance and GPAs.

Lab TA — Position Overview & Expectations

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must have successfully completed ECE 2280 and ECE 2285
  • Minimum GPA of 3.3 (B+) in both the course and the lab
  • Strong subject knowledge to support students in lab activities
  • Effective communication and mentoring skills in MATLAB, LTspice, and circuit construction
  • Familiarity with Thevenin equivalents, Bode plots, Diodes, BJTs, and MOSFETs

Responsibilities

  • Support the professor during lab sessions (3 hrs/week)
  • Assist students with hands-on learning activities during labs
  • Provide clear, constructive peer feedback on lab activities
  • Monitor and report issues with lab equipment, computers, and software
  • Set weekly office hours to help students with course-related questions

Time Commitment — 5 Hours Per Week

⏰ 3 hrs — Lab session (Fri 9:00 am–12:00 pm) ⏰ 1 hr — Office hours ⏰ 1 hr — Prep & meetings with professor

Supplemental Instruction TA — Overview & Expectations

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must have successfully completed ECE 2280
  • Minimum GPA of 3.3 (B+) in the course
  • Strong subject knowledge to support students in learning activities
  • Familiarity with Thevenin equivalents, Bode plots, Diodes, BJTs, and MOSFETs

Responsibilities

  • Set weekly office hours to support students with course-related questions
